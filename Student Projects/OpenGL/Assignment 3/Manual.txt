[OpenGL Application]

- Right click anywhere in the window and choose a shape, defined by its color, from the menu

* F5 for automatically rotating shpaes
* F6 for specifying motion when using arrows
|- alternatively, we may use { I, K, J, L, U, N } , small or capital letters, for moving the objects around
	they are mapped as following, -Y, +Y, -X, +X, -Z, +Z respictively
	
* (W, S, A, D, Q, Z) for rotating the objects, which are mapped as following;
	-Y, +Y, -X, +X, -Z, +Z respictively
